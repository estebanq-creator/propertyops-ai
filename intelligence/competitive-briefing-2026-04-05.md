## Executive Summary

Competitive landscape shows aggressive AI bundling and tiered pricing strategies targeting the 50–500 unit segment. Key finding: **AI features are no longer premium add-ons** — competitors are embedding them in mid-tier plans to reduce churn and increase perceived value.

**PropertyOps AI Positioning Opportunity:**
- AppFolio's minimums ($280–$1,500/month) create pricing cliffs for portfolios under 200 units
- Buildium locks AI behind $400/month Premium tier
- DoorLoop now bundles EliseAI at $1.09–$1.49/unit (aggressive for 25+ units)
- **Gap:** Self-managed landlords (5–50 units) remain underserved — too small for AppFolio efficiency, too large for spreadsheets
