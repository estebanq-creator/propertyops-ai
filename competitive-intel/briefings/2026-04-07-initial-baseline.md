## Executive Summary

- **AppFolio** dominates enterprise segment ($280-$375/mo minimum + $1.40-$3/unit) with AI tools in Plus tier; too expensive for our 5-150 unit target
- **Buildium** mid-market leader ($62-$400/mo) launched **Lumina AI** suite: tenant communication, maintenance coordination, workflow automation, AI-assisted writing
- **RentRedi/TenantCloud/Avail** serve DIY landlords ($0-$60/mo) but lack sophisticated AI—gap opportunity for PropertyOps AI
- **Critical Finding:** No competitor offers *forensic document analysis without scoring* (Laura's positioning)—all AI screening tools produce risk scores/recommendations (Fair Housing liability exposure)
- **Tony Opportunity:** Maintenance automation exists but requires human approval workflows—none position "AI does prep, human decides" as core feature

## Competitor Landscape

### Tier 1: Enterprise (Not Our Target)

#### AppFolio
**Pricing:**
- Core: $280/mo minimum + $1.40/unit/mo
- Plus: $375/mo minimum + $3/unit/mo
- Example: 100 units = $280/mo (Core), $675/mo (Plus)

**Key Features:**
- Online rent collection, maintenance tracking, resident portal
- Plus tier: AI tools, advanced reporting, workflow management, performance insights
- AppFolio Stack™ (premium add-on)

**Positioning:** Professional property managers, 250+ units
**Threat Level:** LOW (pricing excludes our target market)

---

### Tier 2: Mid-Market (Overlap Risk)

#### Buildium
**Pricing:**
- Essential: $62/mo starting (scales per-unit)
- Growth: $192/mo
- Premium: $400/mo
- Add-ons: Maintenance Contact Center ($110/unit + $300 setup), Leasing Contact Center ($285/unit + $300 setup)

**Key Features:**
- Core accounting, maintenance, tenant screening, online portals
- **Lumina AI (2026 launch):**
  - AI Assistant for guidance/walkthroughs
  - AI Bill Scan for invoice data extraction
  - Write with AI for resident messages/emails (tone/translation)
  - Available on Growth/Premium tiers only

**Positioning:** Growing portfolios, 50-500 units
**Threat Level:** MEDIUM (Lumina AI overlaps with Tony's maintenance triage, but lacks forensic document analysis)

**Strategic Note:** Buildium's AI is *assistive* not *analytical*—they don't do document integrity forensics (Laura's domain)

---

### Tier 3: DIY Landlord (Our Phase 1 Target)

#### RentRedi
**Pricing:** $1/unit/mo (min $12/mo) or $19.95/mo flat; $199/year
**Features:** Rent collection, tenant screening, texting, leases, maintenance requests
**AI Capabilities:** None identified
**Best For:** 1-25 units, mobile-first landlords

#### TenantCloud
**Pricing:** Free for 1 property (up to 75 units); Pro $15-18/mo; Growth $29-60/mo
**Features:** Credit/background checks, income verification, lease default risk analysis
**AI Capabilities:** Risk scoring (Fair Housing concern—produces recommendations)
**Best For:** Scaling portfolios 1-75+ units

#### Avail (Zillow Group)
**Pricing:** Free up to 3 units; Essential $29/mo (10 units); Pro $59/mo (25 units)
**Features:** Listings syndication, free ACH, tenant screening ($39-49 pay-per-use)
**AI Capabilities:** None identified
**Best For:** Beginners, 1-10 units

## AI Feature Competitive Analysis

### Tenant Screening AI

| Competitor | Approach | Fair Housing Risk |
|------------|----------|-------------------|
| RealPage AI Screening | Predictive scoring from 30M lease outcomes | HIGH (scores + recommendations) |
| TenantCloud | Lease default risk analysis | MEDIUM-HIGH (risk outputs) |
| Braiin | Automated background/credit checks | MEDIUM (unclear if scoring) |
| **Laura (PropertyOps AI)** | **Forensic document integrity ONLY** | **LOW (no scoring, no recommendations)** |

**Key Differentiator:** Laura's constraint (analysis without scoring) is a *compliance feature*, not a limitation. We can market this as "Fair Housing Safe AI."

### Maintenance Automation AI

| Competitor | Capabilities | Human Approval Required? |
|------------|--------------|--------------------------|
| Buildium Lumina | AI-assisted writing, bill scan | Unclear |
| Braiin | AI-driven inspections, vendor coordination | Unclear |
| Second Nature | Predictive maintenance | N/A (predictions only) |
| **Tony (PropertyOps AI)** | **Triage, communication drafting, work order prep** | **YES (explicit in Phase 1-2)** |

**Key Differentiator:** Tony's explicit human-approval workflow is a *trust feature* for Phase 1-2. We should emphasize "AI prepares, you decide" in messaging.
