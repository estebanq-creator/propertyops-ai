# PropertyOps AI Product Release Sequence

**Decision Date:** 2026-04-09  
**Purpose:** Single source of truth for what ships first, what is gated, and what is explicitly not part of the first launch.

## Decision

**Laura ships first.**

PropertyOps AI's first customer-facing launch is the Laura pilot: forensic document integrity analysis for landlord onboarding, with human review and explicit Fair Housing guardrails.

Tony does **not** ship first. Tony is treated as a **closed beta / operator-assisted workflow** until the approval, habitability, and compliance controls are proven in live pilot use.

Billing does **not** determine Phase 1 launch timing. Early pilots use manual invoicing and founder-led contracting until billing is implemented.

## What Ships First

### Phase 1: Laura Pilot
- Landlord-facing Laura portal
- Forensic-only reports
- Review gate workflow
- Legal disclaimer guardrails
- Human-reviewed outputs only

### Phase 2: Tony Closed Beta
- Draft-only maintenance messaging
- Owner approval queue
- Habitability escalation
- Test tenants / limited beta usage only

### Phase 3: Commercial Enablement
- Billing
- Onboarding workflows
- Production operating motion for broader rollout

### Phase 4: Controlled Autonomy
- Expanded automation only after trust, compliance, and workflow data support it

## Why This Sequence

- Laura is the clearest low-liability wedge and is closer to pilot readiness.
- Tony exists technically, but it carries higher operational and habitability risk.
- Competitive intelligence indicates the 5-150 unit AI-native gap is still open, so speed matters, but only if launch sequencing matches what is actually shippable.
- Billing is not yet implemented and should be handled operationally for pilots rather than treated as the product-sequencing decision.

## Explicit Non-Goals For Phase 1

- No Tony general availability
- No autonomous tenant-landlord messaging
- No billing-led rollout dependency
- No controlled autonomy

## Canonical Answer

If asked, "What ships first?" the answer is:

**Laura ships first as the Phase 1 pilot. Tony follows as a closed beta after compliance and approval workflows are validated. Billing is handled manually for early pilots and is not the Phase 1 blocker.**
