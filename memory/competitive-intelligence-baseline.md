# Competitive Intelligence Baseline - April 5, 2026

## Monitoring Scope
**Targets:** AppFolio, Buildium, DoorLoop, Innago, TenantCloud, Braiin, RealPage
**Focus Areas:** Feature releases, pricing changes, AI/automation capabilities, positioning for 5-150 unit segment

## Competitor Pricing & Positioning (April 2026)

### AppFolio
- **Target:** Medium-to-large portfolios (250+ units typical)
- **Pricing:**
  - Residential Core: $1.40/unit (min $280/mo)
  - Residential Plus: $3/unit (min $1,500/mo)
  - Commercial/Association: $0.80-$1.50/unit (min $280/mo)
- **Key Features:** Full accounting, AI leasing tools, tenant screening, automated workflows, tenant portals, maintenance tracking, API access (premium)
- **Positioning:** Enterprise-grade, comprehensive, high minimums exclude small landlords
- **Weakness for Our Segment:** Pricing prohibitive for <250 units; minimums create dead zones

### Buildium
- **Target:** Mid-to-large portfolios (100-500+ units)
- **Pricing:**
  - Essential: $58-$62/mo (up to ~150 units)
  - Growth: $183-$192/mo
  - Premium: $375-$400/mo
- **Key Features:** Core accounting, online payments, tenant portals, maintenance tracking, Lumina AI® (bill scan, message generation, guidance), eLeasing, TransUnion screening
- **Positioning:** Best-in-class financial management, strong accounting focus
- **Weakness for Our Segment:** Still expensive for 5-50 unit landlords; AI features gated to higher tiers

### DoorLoop
- **Target:** Small-to-mid landlords (5-50+ units)
- **Pricing:**
  - Starter: $49-$69/mo (up to 20 units)
  - Pro: $79-$119/mo
  - Premium: $109-$169/mo
  - Scaling: +$20-$40 per additional 20 units
- **Key Features:** Full accounting, leasing, maintenance, transparent tiers, demo available
- **Positioning:** Full-featured at lowest cost for comprehensive needs
- **Threat Level:** **HIGH** - Direct competitor for our 5-150 unit sweet spot

### Innago
- **Target:** Budget-conscious small landlords
- **Pricing:**
  - Starter: $9/mo (AI lease analysis, rent collection, portal)
  - Professional: $39/mo (full accounting, maintenance, team)
  - Enterprise: $399/mo
  - Free tier available
- **Key Features:** Flat-rate pricing (not per-unit), AI lease analysis, rent collection
- **Positioning:** Lowest-cost entry point with AI features
- **Threat Level:** **MEDIUM** - Undercuts on price but narrower feature set

### TenantCloud
- **Target:** Small-to-large portfolios
- **Pricing:** Not clearly published (est. free-$20/unit/mo; Rentec Direct comparable at $45-$65/mo for 10-50 units)
- **Key Features:** Credit/background checks, lease default analysis, QuickBooks/Stripe integration
- **Positioning:** Basic to mid-tier needs
- **Threat Level:** **LOW-MEDIUM** - Less clear positioning, data incomplete

### Braiin
- **Target:** Full rental ecosystem
- **Pricing:** Not published (custom quotes)
- **Key Features:** Listings/enquiry management, screening/verification, inspections/maintenance workflows, financial tracking
- **Positioning:** AI-native full-stack automation
- **Threat Level:** **MEDIUM** - New entrant, AI-first positioning similar to ours

### RealPage
- **Target:** Enterprise/large portfolios
- **Pricing:** Custom quotes
- **Key Features:** AI screening with 30M+ lease data, predictive risk scoring
- **Positioning:** Enterprise risk assessment
- **Threat Level:** **LOW** - Above our segment

## AI Feature Landscape (2026)

### Common AI Capabilities Across Competitors
1. **Tenant Screening Automation**
   - Credit, background, identity checks
   - Rental history analysis
   - Lease default risk prediction (RealPage, TenantCloud)

2. **Maintenance Automation**
   - AI-driven inspections
   - Urgency prioritization for work orders
   - Automated vendor coordination
   - Predictive maintenance forecasting (Braiin, DoorLoop/EliseAI)

3. **Administrative AI**
   - AI-drafted communications (Buildium Lumina, AppFolio Realm-X)
   - Invoice/bill scanning and extraction (Buildium Premium, DoorLoop)
   - Automated resident emails (AppFolio)
   - 24/7 chatbot inquiries (multiple)

4. **Financial Automation**
   - Automated rent collection with late fees
   - Bank reconciliation
   - Budgeting and forecasting
   - 1099 filing (Buildium)

### Key Differentiators
- **Buildium Lumina AI®:** Real-time assistant, tone/translation, bill scan (Premium tier only)
- **AppFolio Realm-X:** Automated resident communications
- **RealPage:** 30M+ lease data for predictive scoring
- **Braiin:** Full ecosystem AI from listing to financials
- **Innago:** AI lease analysis at $9/mo (lowest-cost AI feature)
- **DoorLoop + EliseAI:** Maintenance triage, invoice extraction

## Strategic Implications for PropertyOps AI

### Our Positioning Advantages
1. **Phase Discipline:** Laura (forensic analysis, no scoring) avoids Fair Housing triggers that competitors may not address explicitly
2. **Tony (Operational Layer):** Human-in-the-loop by design through Phase 2 - competitors push "automation" without clear approval workflows
3. **Target Segment Focus:** 5-150 units is underserved (AppFolio/Buildium too expensive, Innago too limited)
4. **Compliance Posture:** Explicit Fair Housing and PAM/PII enforcement as a feature, not afterthought

### Competitive Gaps We Can Exploit
1. **AppFolio/Buildium:** Pricing excludes our target; complexity overwhelms small landlords
2. **DoorLoop:** Full-featured but not AI-native; no forensic document integrity layer
3. **Innago:** Low price but narrow scope; no operational workflow depth
4. **Braiin:** AI-native but unproven; no clear compliance framing
5. **All Competitors:** None explicitly separate forensic analysis (Laura) from operational execution (Tony) with human approval gates

### Pricing Strategy Insights
- **Sweet Spot:** $50-$150/mo for 5-50 units (DoorLoop range)
- **Differentiation:** Value-based pricing on risk reduction (fraud exposure, compliance) vs. per-unit fees
- **Entry Point:** Need sub-$50/mo tier to compete with Innago's $9/mo psychological anchor

### Monitoring Priorities
1. **DoorLoop:** Watch for AI feature additions, pricing changes, EliseAI integration depth
2. **Buildium:** Track Lumina AI feature expansion to lower tiers
3. **Innago:** Monitor feature creep into operational workflows
4. **Braiin:** Track market traction, customer reviews, pricing disclosure
5. **New Entrants:** Watch for AI-native proptech startups targeting 5-150 unit segment
