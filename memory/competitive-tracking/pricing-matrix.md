**Target Market**: 50-500+ units (mid-market focus)

### Current Pricing (2026)

No explicit Q1/Q2 2026 pricing changes announced. Current structure:

| Plan | Starting Price | Unit Limit | Key Features |
|------|----------------|------------|--------------|
| **Essential** | ~$62/mo | ≤20 units | Core accounting, payments, maintenance |
| **Growth** | ~$192/mo | ≤100 units | Essential + eSignatures, analytics, phone support |
| **Premium** | ~$400/mo | Unlimited | Growth + API, priority support, cashback |

### Lumina AI Suite (2026 Launch)

**NEW**: Buildium's agentic AI platform integrated directly into the ecosystem:
- **Tenant Communication Automation**: AI drafting responses
- **Maintenance Coordination**: Automated work order routing
- **Workflow Automation**: Rule-based task triggers
- **AI-Assisted Writing**: Content generation for notices, emails

**Strategic Note**: No separate pricing announced for Lumina AI—bundled into existing tiers. This represents a **doubling down on AI capabilities** without price increases, potentially defensive positioning vs. AppFolio's add-on model.

**Additional Costs**:
- Payment processing: Standard fees apply
- Modules/add-ons: Can increase effective cost
- Onboarding: Requires sales quote

**Key Insight**: Buildium maintaining price stability while adding AI features. Flat-rate predictability remains advantage for 100-300 unit portfolios. Accounting depth is core differentiator.

**Source**: Buildium 2026 product reviews, managecasa.com comparison (April 2026)

## Summary: Competitive Pricing Gaps

### Sweet Spot Opportunity (5-150 units)

| Competitor | Gap |
|------------|-----|
| **AppFolio** | Minimum fees make it 3-10x too expensive for <200 units |
| **Buildium** | Viable at 50-150 units ($58-192/mo), but accounting-focused, not AI-native |
| **DoorLoop** | Closest competitor in SMB space, but still traditional SaaS pricing |
| **TenantCloud** | Lacks sophistication for serious 50+ unit operators |
| **Avail** | DIY tool, not operations platform |
| **RealPage** | Overkill + Fair Housing liability from scoring |

### PropertyOps AI Pricing Advantage

If we price Starter tier at ~$199/mo for 20 units:
- **vs. AppFolio**: $199 vs. $280 minimum (29% savings) + human oversight included
- **vs. Buildium**: $199 vs. $183-192 (comparable) + AI agent workforce included
- **vs. DoorLoop**: $199 vs. ~$199 (parity) + forensic analysis differentiation

At 100 units with full agent fleet (~$799/mo):
- **vs. AppFolio**: $799 vs. $900-1,500 (11-47% savings)
- **vs. Buildium**: $799 vs. $375-400 (premium, but justified by agent workforce)
- **Value prop**: Not cheaper SaaS—cheaper *labor* (replaces VA/bookkeeper/maintenance coordinator time)
