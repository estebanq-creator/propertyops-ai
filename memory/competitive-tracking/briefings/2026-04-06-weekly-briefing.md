## Competitor Monitoring

### AppFolio

**Pricing Status:** No changes detected  
**Current Pricing (per Sept 2022 baseline):**
- Core: $1.40/unit/mo ($280 minimum)
- Plus: $3.00/unit/mo ($1,500 minimum)
- Commercial/Association: $0.80-$1.50/unit/mo ($280 minimum)

**Strategic Moves:**
- Revenue mix shifting to high-margin add-ons (Resident Onboarding Lift, premium tiers)
- 20% sales growth vs 8% user growth = deeper per-unit monetization
- Targeting $1.1B revenue, 26.5% operating margin by 2026
- AI features (leasing, maintenance, reporting) deployed but not priced separately

**Implications for PropertyOps:**
- ✅ **Validation:** AppFolio's add-on strategy confirms landlords will pay for targeted value
- ✅ **Wedge Opportunity:** $280 minimum remains barrier for <250 unit landlords — our target segment
- ⚠️ **Watch:** AI features bundled into premium tiers — could commoditize if they launch standalone forensic analysis

**Monitoring Cadence:** Weekly pricing page checks, quarterly earnings call review

### Buildium

**Pricing Status:** No changes detected  
**Current Pricing:**
- Essential: $62/mo (no AI features)
- Growth: $192/mo (Write with AI, reduced transaction fees)
- Premium: $400/mo (full Lumina AI Suite)

**Lumina AI Features (Premium only):**
- AI Assistant (in-platform guidance)
- AI Bill Scan (invoice data extraction)
- Write with AI (message/email generation)
- Conversational AI support
- Maintenance triage automation
- Workflow automation

**Strategic Moves:**
- AI positioned as "trusted extension" — emphasizes human oversight (aligns with our Phase 1-2 constraint model)
- AI features used as Premium tier differentiator ($400/mo vs $62/mo)
- Targeting 50-500 unit managers — above our 5-150 unit focus

**Implications for PropertyOps:**
- ✅ **Validation:** AI features command premium pricing — landlords will pay for automation
- ✅ **Differentiation:** Lumina focuses on routine tasks (bills, messages), not forensic document integrity
- ⚠️ **Watch:** AI Bill Scan is adjacent to Laura's document analysis — but focused on invoices, not tenant screening docs

**Monitoring Cadence:** Weekly feature announcements, pricing page checks
