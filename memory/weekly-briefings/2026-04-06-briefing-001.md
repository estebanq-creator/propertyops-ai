## Competitor Movements

### 🚨 DoorLoop — AI Inspections Launch (Feb 10, 2026)
**What Changed:** New mobile-first inspection feature automating photo capture, report generation, and work order creation.

**Capabilities:**
- Guided on-site capture with structured room-by-room flow
- AI organization of photos/notes, automatic issue flagging
- Real-time compliant PDF reports with timestamps and geolocation
- Instant bulk work order creation from findings
- Integrates with DoorLoop's existing AI Assistant (handles 80% of tenant requests)

**Pricing:** Custom demo quote; platform scales from ~$69/month

**Implication for PropertyOps:** This is the closest direct competitor feature to Tony's maintenance triage + inspection workflow. DoorLoop is now offering end-to-end inspection → work order automation. Our differentiation must emphasize:
- Forensic document integrity (Laura) as upstream risk detection
- Human-in-the-loop approval enforcement (Phase 1-2 constraint as feature)
- Fair Housing compliance posture (DoorLoop makes no such claims)

**Monitoring Priority:** HIGH — Track adoption signals, customer reviews, pricing changes
