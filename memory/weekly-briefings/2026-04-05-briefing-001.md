## Strategic Implications

### PropertyOps AI Differentiators (Validated)
1. **Phase Discipline:** Laura (forensic analysis, no scoring) + Tony (human approval gates) remains unique vs. competitors pushing "automation" without compliance framing.
2. **Target Segment:** 5-150 units remains underserved — AppFolio/Buildium too expensive, Innago too limited, DoorLoop best competitor but not AI-native.
3. **Compliance Posture:** No competitor explicitly frames Fair Housing/PAM/PII enforcement as core feature.

### Pricing Strategy Validation
- **Innago free tier:** Creates psychological anchor at $0, but feature gap justifies $50-150/mo value pricing.
- **DoorLoop range:** $69-199/mo confirms our target pricing is competitive for 5-50 units.
- **Buildium Premium:** $400/mo for AI features validates willingness-to-pay for operational AI when tied to outcomes.

### Risks to Monitor
1. **Braiin pricing:** If they disclose sub-$100/mo pricing with similar AI scope, threat level increases to HIGH.
2. **DoorLoop AI additions:** Any AI feature announcements matching Laura/Tony capabilities require immediate escalation.
3. **Buildium Lumina expansion:** If AI features move to Essential/Growth tiers, competitive pressure increases for 50-150 unit segment.
